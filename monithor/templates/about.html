About monithor

Initially this application was without UI but it was difficult to maintain the lists of known and unknown mac addresses. Finally choosed to try something I haven't tried before, Django. It was an interesting journey :-)
To use this tool you would need a network node (preferably in the center of your network) that responds to SNMP. This is needed to collect the mac addresses active in your network. You would also need an account at maclookup (https://my.maclookup.app/login) to make it easier to identify unknown mac addresses. An account with pushover (https://pushover.net/login) is also needed to send you notifications when new mac addresses show up in your network.
Keep your network safe!
